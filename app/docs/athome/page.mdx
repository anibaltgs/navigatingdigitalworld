![nextdns_disable1](/nextdns_disable1.gif)

## Ads blocking:

You should install an ad blocker on your computer. Even FBI recomends it: https://www.ic3.gov/Media/Y2022/PSA221221?=8324278624
Not having an adblock installed is the gateway for phising, malware, scams and even virus.
This extension will also tackle ads embeded in. Ie: the Ads you see in YouTube.

This is a must have.

On Chrome: https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/ublock-origin/cjpalhdlnbpafiamejdnhcphjbkeiagm?hl=en
On Safari: https://adguard.com/en/adguard-safari/overview.html
